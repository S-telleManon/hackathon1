Store Keeper

Hidden company Ltd

Rs 20,000 - 30,000
Added 20/07/2025
Black River
Deadline 20/08/2025

Job Purpose:
To efficiently manage the receipt, storage, and issuance of goods and materials, ensuring accurate inventory records and timely supply to internal departments.

Key Responsibilities:
– Receive, inspect, and verify incoming shipments against purchase orders and delivery notes.
– Organize and store materials in designated locations to optimize space and accessibility.
– Issue goods and materials to authorized personnel based on approved requisitions.
– Maintain accurate and up-to-date inventory records using manual or computerized systems.
– Conduct regular stock counts and assist with periodic inventory audits.
– Report any discrepancies, damages, or shortages promptly to management.
– Ensure the store area is clean, safe, and complies with company and safety regulations.
– Coordinate with procurement and logistics teams to ensure timely replenishment of stock.

Required Skills & Qualifications:
– Previous experience in storekeeping, warehouse, or inventory management is preferred.
– Basic knowledge of inventory control procedures and stock management systems.
– Good organizational and record-keeping skills.
– Ability to work independently and as part of a team.
– Physical ability to handle and move goods when necessary.
– Attention to detail and accuracy in documentation.
– Basic computer skills for inventory tracking.
