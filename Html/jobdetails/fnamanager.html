Finance and Admin Manager

Supercar Ltee

Rs 40,000 - 50,000
Added 01/08/2025
Flacq
Deadline 31/08/2025

Job Purpose:
To oversee and manage the company’s financial operations and administrative functions, ensuring accurate financial reporting, compliance, and efficient support to all departments.

Key Responsibilities:
– Supervise day-to-day accounting activities, including accounts payable, receivable, payroll, and general ledger.
– Prepare monthly, quarterly, and annual financial statements and management reports.
– Monitor budgets and provide analysis on variances and financial performance.
– Ensure compliance with tax, legal, and statutory requirements.
– Manage cash flow, banking relationships, and financial forecasting.
– Oversee administrative functions, including office management, procurement, and record-keeping.
– Develop and implement internal controls, policies, and procedures to enhance operational efficiency.
– Support external audits and coordinate with auditors and external consultants as required.
– Lead, mentor, and supervise the finance and admin team to achieve departmental goals.

Required Skills & Qualifications:
– Degree in accounting, finance, or a related field; professional qualification (e.g., ACCA, ACA) preferred.
– Proven experience in finance and administrative management roles.
– Strong knowledge of financial regulations, accounting standards, and compliance requirements.
– Excellent analytical, problem-solving, and organizational skills.
– Proficiency in accounting software and Microsoft Office tools.
– Strong leadership and communication skills to manage teams and collaborate across departments.
– Ability to handle confidential information with integrity.
