Import Support Clerk

ABZ Company Ltd

Rs Negotiable
Added 01/08/2025
Moka
Deadline 31/08/2025
Permanent

Job Purpose:
The Import Support Clerk plays a key role in ensuring smooth and timely customs clearance of imported goods. This position focuses on accurately communicating the amount payable for customs clearance to relevant stakeholders and performing precise data entry into internal systems to support the logistics and finance teams.

Key Responsibilities:
- Communicate clearly and promptly the amount payable for customs duties, taxes, and other clearance fees to the accounting team and other concerned departments.
- Perform accurate and timely data entry of import documentation and clearance details into the companyâ€™s ERP and other logistics systems.
- Check and validate invoices and clearance documents to ensure consistency and compliance with company procedures.
- Coordinate with customs brokers, freight forwarders, and other third parties to track shipments and support the clearance process.
- Maintain organized digital and physical records of import files and related correspondence.
- Assist in preparing reports on import activities and associated costs for internal use.
- Support the import team in resolving any documentation discrepancies or clearance issues.

Required Skills & Qualifications:
- Certificate or diploma in logistics, business administration, accounting, or a related field.
- Previous experience in import/export operations, logistics, or data entry is preferred.
- Strong attention to detail and high level of accuracy in data entry.
- Basic understanding of customs clearance processes is an advantage.
- Proficient in Microsoft Office (Excel, Word) and familiar with ERP or logistics software.
- Good communication skills, both written and verbal.
- Ability to prioritize tasks and work under tight deadlines.

Why Join ABZ Company?
- Be part of a collaborative and professional logistics team.
- Contribute to the smooth flow of international trade operations.
- Opportunities for growth and learning in a dynamic industry.
